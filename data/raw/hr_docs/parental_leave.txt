PARENTAL LEAVE POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Department: Human Resources
Version: 2.1

1. PURPOSE
This policy outlines parental leave entitlements for all employees following the birth, adoption, or fostering of a child.

2. ELIGIBILITY
Employees who have completed six months of continuous employment are eligible. Both primary and secondary caregivers are covered.

3. ENTITLEMENTS

3.1 Primary Caregiver
Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave. An additional 8 weeks of unpaid leave may be taken immediately following the paid period upon written request.

3.2 Secondary Caregiver
Secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks of fully paid parental leave, to be taken within the first 12 months following the birth or placement of the child.

4. NOTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
Employees must notify their line manager and HR at least 8 weeks before the intended start of leave via the HR portal.

5. RETURN TO WORK
Employees returning from parental leave are entitled to return to their original role at the same grade and salary.

6. CONTACT
For questions contact hr@company.com or speak with your HR Business Partner.
